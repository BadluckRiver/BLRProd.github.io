  • Professional Background

    • After High school, BLR joined the military, and worked as a Lab Technician for his duration of his service.
    His first post landed him in the Microbiology field. BLR has been in the Microbiology Research field for almost 10 years.
    While working as a research technician, he realized that it was not truly his passion, and needed to find another career path.
    He spent 5 years in the military, working on a microbiology lab technician, and almost another 5 as a contractor for the same lab.
    He has been published a few times as an author. He has worked with over 100,000 microbiology samples from around the world, testing them for
    identification, and antimicrobial susceptibilities, building a repository for said samples, and cataloging their mutations over time.
    That is a basic summary of his previous professional history. Again, all of this did not continue to spark his interest, so he chose learn
    something else that did.

  • Educational Background

    • With his education benefits, he began to attend school for Digital Media and Web Design, due to his expansive interest in everything
    on a screen. It amazed him how much goes into what someone sees on their computer, mobile, and even tv screen, especially when it comes
    to video games. He has taken courses that expand his understanding on Adobe platforms, video game design, web design, and portfolio creation.
    He has also taken courses within the realm of computer science, such as troubleshooting, networking, and cloud computing. These classes were taken
    to broaden his understanding of the inner workings of his down devices.

  • Artistic Background

    • Throughout his life, BLR has had more of a creative mind. He loves to take things apart, and piece them back together, bit by bit to see how things work.
    He has also taken art courses to help him with his goals to further create designs, or elements to be viewed on the screen by others.
    As a kid, he loved to draw graffiti, and push his imaginary boundaries by transforming plain text into something less plain. Although
    he has not made graffiti in quite some time, he does view it as an ultimate art form, which many people may not.
    His dream is to one day work on a game he is proud of or has enjoyed throughout his life. An example of that, would be
    Old School Runescape.
