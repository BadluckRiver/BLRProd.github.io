BLR's Future Endeavor Ideas:
  • Content Creation:

    • BLR intends to start a youtube channel in the future (Estimated Time Frame: Not yet decided). This channel will
    primarily serve as a hobby project, to catalog his love for video games, and the history of his adventures. Think of it
    as a vintage to modern gaming channel, where he will go back through the catalog of games he has started throughout his life
    but never got around to finishing them. He has a wide array of titles that fit this category, dating all the way back to the Era of Super Nintendo.
    If this sounds interesting to you, please feel free to send a contact to let him know that this is something you would be interested in watching.
    With some support, he will surely be pushed to get it started sooner, rather than later.

  • Need Emojis?

    • BLR has been viewing content creators for quite a while, hence his desire to start his own channel. He also has had some
    friends start streaming their own content, and he was approached to create emojis for them. It may seem like a small thing,
    but emojis are used in chats more often than actual text it seems. If this is something you are interested in for your own
    creations, or streams and chats, feel free to contact BLR for options and ideas.

  • Video Editing (Coming Soon)

    • In the same vein as the previous points, BLR would like to begin helping others edit their videos and content, or
    assist with clipping streams to make videos from them for other creators. It will take some practice, but BLR is looking
    to expand his portfolio with more abilities. He has not done video editing in some time, but it may be in the cards for him soon.
